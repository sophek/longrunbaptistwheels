DOES CAFFEINE TEMPT PEOPLE TO BECOME ANGRIER? "Caffeine may be linked to violence - The taoiseach has asked police to investigate the possible link between street violence involving young men and their consumption of stimulant drinks, it emerged yesterday...Irish sales of Red Bull the leading stimulant drink brand containing caffeine and other ingredients will exceed 14 million cans this year..." (The Irish News Ltd) Comments: A Christian man who is constantly yelling at his wife or children, throwing fits in traffic, or getting stressed-out and overly-irritated at ordinary, daily vexations, might want to examine the effect that coffee, tea or cola is having upon his body and nerves. Caffeine might be increasing the temptation to be sinfully angry or irritated. I have heard testimonies from people who have reduced stress by switching to healthy alternatives such as water, vegetable juice, soy or dandelion-root coffees [these are healthy, and caffeine-free], etc. It might be very helpful in your own life to break the caffeine (or for some, nicotine) addictions. It has been a blessing in my life: Proverbs 15:18 A wrathful man stirreth up strife: but he that is slow to anger appeaseth strife. James 1:20 For the wrath of man worketh not the righteousness of God. Matthew 5:22 But I say unto you, That whosoever is angry with his brother without a cause shall be in danger of the judgment...
