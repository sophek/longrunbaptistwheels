(Sermon, Joey Faust) *************************************************************** Luke 18:17 Verily I say unto you, Whosoever shall not RECEIVE THE KINGDOM as a little child shall in no wise enter therein. 18 And a certain ruler asked him, saying, Good Master, what shall I do to inherit eternal life? 19 And Jesus said unto him, Why callest thou me good? none is good, save one, that is, God. 20 Thou knowest the commandments, Do not commit adultery, Do not kill, Do not steal, Do not bear false witness, Honour thy father and thy mother. 22 Now when Jesus heard these things, he said unto him, Yet lackest thou one thing: sell all that thou hast, and distribute unto the poor, and thou shalt have treasure in heaven: and come, follow me. 23 And when he heard this, HE WAS VERY SORROWFUL: for he was very rich. 24 And when Jesus saw that he was very sorrowful, he said, How hardly shall they that have riches ENTER INTO THE KINGDOM OF GOD! The Lord wants to give us the Millennial Kingdom. He wants us to receive it from Him. However, He does not give it in the exact manner that He gives salvation in eternity. For example, there were times in the OT where God commanded the Israelites to stand still and see the salvation of the Lord: Exodus 14:13 And Moses said unto the people, Fear ye not, STAND STILL, and see the salvation of the LORD, which he will shew to you to day: for the Egyptians whom ye have seen to day, ye shall see them again no more for ever. 14 THE LORD SHALL FIGHT FOR YOU, AND YE SHALL HOLD YOUR PEACE. 24 And it came to pass, that in the morning watch the LORD looked unto the host of the Egyptians through the pillar of fire and of the cloud, and troubled the host of the Egyptians, 25 And took off their chariot wheels, that they drave them heavily: so that the Egyptians said, Let us flee from the face of Israel; for the LORD FIGHTETH FOR THEM against the Egyptians. 26 And the LORD said unto Moses, Stretch out thine hand over the sea, that the waters may come again upon the Egyptians, upon their chariots, and upon their horsemen. This is what Jesus did for us on the Cross, in obtaining eternal salvation FOR us. It involved NO WORKS of ours. We do not lift a sword, or a shield: Titus 3:5 NOT BY WORKS of righteousness which we have done, but according to his mercy he saved usâ€¦ Ephesians 2:8 For by grace are ye saved through faith; and that not of yourselves: it is the gift of God: 9 NOT OF WORKS, lest any man should boast. Galatians 2:16 Knowing that a man is NOT justified by the WORKS of the lawâ€¦ Galatians 3:10 For as many as are of the works of the law are under the curseâ€¦ Romans 4:5 But to him that WORKETH NOT, but believeth on him that justifieth the ungodly, his faith is counted for righteousness. 6 Even as David also describeth the blessedness of the man, unto whom God imputeth righteousness WITHOUT WORKS, 7 Saying, Blessed are they whose iniquities are forgiven, and whose sins are covered. So we see that eternal salvation is given in a similar manner as Israel defeated the Egyptians: It is totally a work of God ALONE, without any participation of man. As Moses lifted up his wooden rod, so was Jesus lifted up on the wooden Cross: Exodus 14:16 But LIFT THOU UP THY ROD, and stretch out thine hand over the sea, and divide it: and the children of Israel shall go on dry ground through the midst of the sea. John 12:32 And I, IF I BE LIFTED UP from the earth, will draw all men unto me. 33 This he said, signifying what death he should die. On the other hand, salvation into the Millennial Kingdom is given unto us in ANOTHER MANNER. It is true that Jesus teaches in Luke 18:17, that His disciples are to RECEIVE it. But Jesus is talking to His disciples who already possess eternal salvation. They are called to receive the world to come, or the kingdom, in a different manner than they have received the free gift of eternal salvation. In regard to the future Kingdom, they do not simply stand still and see the salvation of the Lord. They are called to go up like Caleb and Joshua were willing to do in regard to the Promised Land, TO FIGHT the giants by the Lord's power. The fact that we must fight (instead of simply standing still and watching), does not take away from the fact that the Millennial Kingdom is something the Lord GIVES to us. For example, in regard to the land of Canaan, the Lord was willing to give the POWER to Israel to slay the giants: Numbers 14:6 And Joshua the son of Nun, and Caleb the son of Jephunneh, which were of them that searched the land, rent their clothes: 7 And they spake unto all the company of the children of Israel, saying, The land, which we passed through to search it, is an exceeding good land. 8 If the LORD delight in us, then HE WILL BRING US INTO THIS LAND, and GIVE it us; a land which floweth with milk and honey. 9 Only rebel not ye against the LORD, neither fear ye the people of the land; for they are bread for us: their defence is departed from them, and the LORD is with us: FEAR THEM NOT. This is exactly what Jesus means in Luke 12, when He says that the Father will GIVE us this Kingdom when we SEEK it: Luke 12:31 But rather SEEK YE THE KINGDOM OF GOD; and all these things shall be added unto you. 32 FEAR NOT, little flock; for it is your Father's good pleasure TO GIVE YOU THE KINGDOM. 33 Sell that ye have, and give alms; provide yourselves bags which wax not old, a treasure in the heavens that faileth not, where no thief approacheth, neither moth corrupteth. 34 For where your treasure is, there will your heart be also. Jesus is giving His disciples the same type of encouraging words that Joshua, a type of Christ, gave to Israel, when he told them not to fear the giants. And when Jesus says that the Father will "give" us the Kingdom, He means what Joshua meant, when he said that the Lord would "give" the children of Israel the land of Canaan. Joshua never meant that the land would necessarily be given without any battle at all! In the same manner, Jesus does not imply that we will be given the future Millennial Kingdom without any warfare at all. Caleb FOUGHT for the land by the Lord power: Numbers 14:24 But my servant CALEB, because he had another spirit with him, and hath followed me fully, HIM WILL I BRING INTO THE LAND whereinto he went; and his seed shall possess it. Joshua 14:10 And now, behold, the LORD hath kept me [Caleb] alive, as he said, these forty and five years, even since the LORD spake this word unto Moses, while the children of Israel wandered in the wilderness: and now, lo, I am this day fourscore and five years old. 11 As yet I am as strong this day as I was in the day that Moses sent me: as my strength was then, even so IS MY STRENGTH NOW, FOR WAR, both to go out, and to come in. 12 Now therefore GIVE me this mountain, whereof the LORD spake in that day; for thou heardest in that day how the Anakims were there, and that the cities were great and fenced: if so be THE LORD WILL BE WITH ME, then I shall be able to drive them out, as the LORD said. 13 And Joshua blessed him, and GAVE unto Caleb the son of Jephunneh Hebron for an inheritance. Joshua 15:14 And Caleb DROVE THENCE the three sons of Anak, Sheshai, and Ahiman, and Talmai, the children of Anak. The Lord accomplished the redemption of Israel without Israel's active warfare. Yet, the Lord accomplished Israel's possession of the Promised Land THROUGH Israel's warfare. The first event pictures the eternal salvation of a sinner. The second event pictures the Millennial salvation of a son of God (i.e. a believer). The attempt by many preachers and theologians to blend these two differing things into one creates a mess. I actually heard one (so-called) fundamental Baptist preacher attempt to combine these two things by teaching that the passages that say "without works" do not refer to MORAL works! Thus, he teaches that we are eternally saved "without works" as long as we define "works" to be Mosaic ceremonies or sacraments, etc.! There are many other "devices" used by these teachers who erroneously attempt to blend these two things that are meant to be kept distinct. But these attempts all end in absurdity and confusion. The Buddha "no thinks, no thoughts." Yet, true, Bible Christianity is not a maze of contradictions. Therefore, if we will understand the Bible, we must keep certain things distinct: TWO AGES: 1. The Millennium (Future Kingdom, or World to Come) 2. The Eternal Age (after the Millennium) TWO VICTORIES: 1. By Faith Alone (without Works) 2. By Faith and Patience (Works) TWO RIGHTEOUSNESSES: 1. Positional (Imputed TO us, once and for all) 2. Practical (Infused, or Worked IN us, daily) 1. Impute: To place to one's account. 2. Infuse: To instil; to fill, as by pouring into. 2 Timothy 2:15 Study to shew thyself approved unto God, a workman that needeth not to be ashamed, RIGHTLY DIVIDING THE WORD of truth. If we confuse any of these things, it will be disastrous! If we are fighting and struggling for eternal salvation, where we are supposed to be resting, and sitting still, we will be like the many legalists, who do not have absolute assurance: Romans 10:3 For they being ignorant of God's righteousness, and going about to establish their own righteousness, have not submitted themselves unto the righteousness of God. 4 For Christ is the end of the law for righteousness to every one that believeth. Matthew 11:28 Come unto me, all ye that labour and are heavy laden, and I will give you rest. Romans 3:21 But now the righteousness of God WITHOUT THE LAW is manifestedâ€¦ Romans 5:1 Therefore being justified by faith, WE HAVE PEACE WITH GOD through our Lord Jesus Christ: There are many legalists who are working for assurance of eternal salvation! They are NOT resting. Because they have NOT SUBMITTED themselves to God's IMPUTED righteousness, given in Christ, they have no peace with God. Their works are DEAD WORKS. The are smiting the Rock twice! They are going in the way of Cain (their own works without resting in the Blood of the Lamb). These religionists see only ONE age to come. They confuse verses concerning the 1000-year Kingdom with verses concerning ETERNITY. They confuse imputed righteousness with infused righteousness. They confuse the PRIZE of entrance into the Millennial Kingdom (through faith and works), with the FREE GIFT of eternal salvation (through faith alone). May the Lord open the eyes of these modern Pharisees to rest in Christ while He may be found!: "If he say that he cannot believe on Jesus Christâ€¦you tell him that believing is no work, but a resting on Jesus Christ." But eternal salvation is not through OUR faithfulness. It is through the faithfulness of Jesus Christ, who perfectly obeyed His Father's will, and submitted Himself to the death of the Cross, and became the Saviour of the world! Woe to the Legalists with their dead works. They attempt to cover their Legalism by sprinkling flowers on the grace of God. They say, "Oh, I do not pretend to be saving myself. It is only GOD'S GRACE that saves me. He performs these good works in me. Without these good works, I will be lost. I thank God that I am being saved by His work through me." Notice, these poor souls are not at rest. They have no absolute assurance of eternal salvation. They are attempting to be saved by the Spirit's work of fruitbearing THROUGH them. They have the cart before the horse. We cannot walk in the Spirit until we live in the Spirit. And we do not live in the Spirit until we REST IN CHRIST'S FINISHED WORK. But let us now change gears. Once we are resting in the imputed righteousness of our Substitute, it is time to fight! In type, the children of Israel came out of the land of Egypt without lifting a sword. But they possessed the Promised Land by constant warfare!: Exodus 17:13 And Joshua discomfited Amalek and his people with the edge of the sword. It is here that we see the second mistake! Once you finally get people out of Egyptian bondage, and resting in their salvation, they begin to complain about the fighting! "Ah, if I have to fight, why didn't I just stay back in Egypt?": Numbers 14:3 And wherefore hath the LORD brought us unto this land, to fall by the sword, that our wives and our children should be a prey? were it not better for us to return into Egypt? Hebrews 3:10 Wherefore I was grieved with that generation, and said, They do alway err in their heart; and they have not known my ways. 11 So I sware in my wrath, They shall not enter into my rest.) 12 Take heed, brethren, lest there be in any of you an evil heart of unbelief, in departing from the living God. Let us manfully cast off the works of darkness. Let us despise the pleasures of sin. There is a kingdom coming. WINTHROP ON THE KING JAMES BIBLE AND FREEDOM (1849) ********************************************************************** Robert Charles Winthrop (1809-1894) was a Representative and a Senator from Massachusetts. The following is part of his address delivered at the Annual Meeting of the Massachusetts Bible Society in Boston, May 28, 1849: "â€¦Mr. President, there is a striking coincidence of dates in the history of our country, and in the history of the Bible. You remember that it was about the year 1607, that King James the First, of blessed memory for this if for nothing else, gave it in charge to fifty or sixty of the most learned ministers of his realm, to prepare that version of the Holy Scriptures, which is NOW EVERYWHERE RECEIVED AND RECOGNIZED AMONG PROTESTANT CHRISTIANS AS THE BIBLE. This version was finally published in 1611, and it is from this event that the general diffusion of the Bible may fairly be said to date. The Bible had, indeed, been more than once previously translated and previously printedâ€¦[But]â€¦however precious the Bible must have been to those who possessed it in those [earlier] days, and however strong the influence which it may have exerted over individual minds, it had little chance to manifest its power over the massesâ€¦.Indeed, the whole number of printed Bibles in existence in Great Britain, up to the commencement of the seventeenth century, is estimated at only about one hundred and seventeen thousand; a little more than one fifth the number distributed by the American Bible Society, and only a little more than one tenth the number distributed by the British and Foreign Bible Society, during the single year last past. It is, thus, ONLY FROM THE PUBLICATION OF THE AUTHORIZED AND STANDARD VERSION OF KING JAMES, THAT THE GENERAL DIFFUSION OF THE HOLY SCRIPTURES CAN BE SAID TO HAVE COMMENCED. IT WAS THEN THAT THE PRINTED WORD OF GOD 'first began to have free course and to be glorified.' AND THAT, YOU REMEMBER, MR. PRESIDENT, WAS THE VERY DATE OF THE EARLIEST SETTLEMENT OF THESE NORTH AMERICAN COLONIES. It was just then, that the Cavaliers were found planting themselves at Jamestown in Virginia; and it was just then, that the Pilgrims, with the Bible in their hands, were seen flying over to Leyden, on their way to our own Plymouth Rock. And now, Sir, IT IS NOT MORE TRUE, IN MY JUDGMENT, THAT THE FIRST SETTLEMENT OF OUR COUNTRY WAS PRECISELY COINCIDENT IN POINT OF TIME, WITH THE PREPARATION AND PUBLICATION OF THIS STANDARD VERSION OF THE BIBLE, THAN IT IS THAT OUR FREE INSTITUTIONS HAVE OWED THEIR SUCCESSFUL RISE AND PROGRESS THUS FAR, and are destined to owe their continued security and improvement in time to come, to the influences which THAT PREPARATION AND PUBLICATION COULD ALONE HAVE PRODUCED. The voice of experience and the voice of reason speak but one language on this point. Both unite in teaching us, that men may as well build their houses upon the sand and expect them to stand, when the rains fall, and the winds blow, and the floods come, as to found free institutions upon any other basis than that morality and virtue, of which the Word of God is the only authoritative rule, and the adequate sanction. All societies of men must be governed in some way or other. The less they may have of stringent State Government, the more they must have of individual self-government. The less they rely on public law or physical force, the more they must rely on private moral restraint. Men, in word, must necessarily be controlled, either by a power within them, or by a power without them; either by the Word of God, or by the strong arm of man; either by the Bible, or by the bayonet. It may do for other countries and other governments to talk about the State supporting religion. Here, under our own free institutions, it is Religion which must support the Stateâ€¦..."
